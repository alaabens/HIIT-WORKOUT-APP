Help

Create Your Interval:

Click on the 'Start a new workout' button to begin crafting your customized interval workout.

  • Provide a descriptive title for your interval to easily identify it later.
  • Add a description for each activity within your interval.
  • Choose the duration for each activity to tailor your workout to your fitness level and goals.

Expand Your Workout:

Feel free to add multiple intervals to your workout routine by clicking the 'Add workout' button.

  • Customize each interval with unique activities and durations to create a diverse and challenging workout session.

Save Your Workout:

Once satisfied with your interval setup, click the 'Add' button to store your personalized HIIT workout.

  • Saved workouts can be accessed later for quick and easy access to your favorite routines through the 'view saved workout' button.

Start Your Workout:

Navigate to the 'Saved' section to view your saved intervals or through 'Start a new workout' button.

  • Select an interval from your saved list and click 'Start' to begin your HIIT workout journey!
